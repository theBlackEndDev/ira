This is a stub for a single customer engagement. Create one directory like this per active or past customer.

## What goes here

Engagement-specific records:

- **`README.md`** (this file) — overview of the engagement: who they are, what you're doing for them, who the contacts are.
- **Contracts, NDAs, MSAs, SOWs** — signed agreements specific to this customer.
- **Deliverables** — drafts and final versions of the work product you're producing for them.
- **Meeting notes** — running log of calls and key decisions.
- **Reference material** they've given you — their docs, their architecture, their data.
- **Anything else specific to this customer** that your DA should be able to surface when you ask "what's the status with $CUSTOMER."

## Privacy

Customer names, contract values, and engagement details are sensitive. This directory is part of your private USER/ tree and is **not** included in PAI public releases.
