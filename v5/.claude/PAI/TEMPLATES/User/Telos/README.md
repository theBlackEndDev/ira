# Telos

**Your purpose, goals, and the strategies that connect them.** The directional content for your life — the "why," the "where," and the "how I'm getting there."

Telos is phase 1 of the Interview skill. Everything else your DA does derives from what's here.

## Files in This Domain

| File | Kind | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| `Mission.md` | narrative | Core mission(s) — your north star(s) |
| `Goals.md` | collection | Active goals with timelines, organized by year |
| `Problems.md` | collection | Problems you're solving in the world |
| `Strategies.md` | collection | Active strategies — how you're attacking the problems |
| `Challenges.md` | narrative | Personal challenges you're working on |
| `Predictions.md` | collection | Dated forecasts about the world |
| `Traumas.md` | narrative | Formative experiences that shape your operating system |
| `Wrong.md` | collection | Things you've been wrong about — a humility record |
| `Learned.md` | collection | Hard-won lessons |
| `Status.md` | reference | Current snapshot: what's active, what's paused, what's blocked |
| `2036.md` | narrative | A day in your life 10 years out — the narrative target |
| `PrincipalTelos.md` | reference | **Auto-generated.** Compressed summary loaded at every session. Do not edit manually. |

## The Flow

1. Start with `Mission.md`. One sentence, then iterate.
2. Write `Problems.md` — what's broken in the world that you care about.
3. Write `Goals.md` — what you're doing about those problems this year.
4. Write `Strategies.md` — how you're attacking the goals.
5. The rest accretes over time.

## Why This Matters

Your DA reads `PrincipalTelos.md` at every session startup. Without it, your DA is generic. With it, every suggestion is filtered through *your* direction — what you're trying to build, what you're trying to avoid, what you're trying to become.

---

*Tip: Telos files are allowed to be loose and evolving. They're not contracts. They're working hypotheses about your direction. Update them as you learn.*
