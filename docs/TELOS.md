# IRA TELOS System

> Life-aware context that makes IRA align with your goals, not just your tasks.

---

## What TELOS Is

TELOS (Telic Evolution and Life Operating System) captures your mission, goals, beliefs, and projects so that IRA can make decisions that align with your actual life direction.

When IRA knows your mission and goals, it can:
- **Score app ideas** against your strategic direction
- **Prioritize features** based on your revenue goals
- **Suggest channels** that match your audience
- **Flag misalignment** when a task contradicts your stated priorities

---

## TELOS Files

Stored in `.ira/telos/` (migrated from PAI) or created fresh:

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `MISSION.md` | Your core mission statement |
| `GOALS.md` | Active goals with timelines and dependencies |
| `BELIEFS.md` | Core beliefs that inform decisions |
| `WISDOM.md` | Lessons learned, mental models |
| `PROJECTS.md` | Active projects and their status |
| `BOOKS.md` | Books that shaped your thinking |

---

## Integration Points

TELOS is not a separate skill — it's integrated into routing:

1. **Forge scoring** — Ideas scored against TELOS goals and beliefs
2. **Refinery channels** — Marketing strategy aligned with audience from TELOS
3. **Architecture decisions** — Technology choices informed by TELOS preferences
4. **Prioritization** — When multiple tasks compete, TELOS goals break the tie

---

## Updating TELOS

```
"Update my goals — PrivacyFinance launch is now priority 1"
"Add to beliefs — subscription software is a betrayal of trust"
"I just finished reading The Mom Test — add to books"
```

IRA updates the corresponding file directly.
